Who We Serve

Three Roles. One Mission.

CoVenture Africa is built around three essential participants in every successful venture. Which one are you?

01
The Investor

Capital Meets Curated Opportunity

You have the capital. CoVenture Africa brings you the vetted ideas, the assembled talent, and the execution. All you need to do is fund what moves you.

  • Browse a private portfolio of pre-vetted ventures
  • Dedicated onboarding with a CoVenture representative
  • Invest in emerging African markets with confidence
  • Hands-off investing with hands-on execution partners
  • Early access to high-potential, underserved opportunities
02
The Entrepreneur

Your Idea. The Right Team Behind It.

You have the vision. CoVenture Africa connects you with the capital and skilled professionals ready to make it real — without diluting your ownership prematurely.

  • Access investors looking for your kind of opportunity
  • Build a team of skilled professionals exchanging skills for equity
  • Structured onboarding to present your venture professionally
  • Your idea stays protected throughout the vetting process
  • A credible platform that elevates your pitch legitimacy
03
The Professional

Trade Skills for a Stake in Something Bigger.

You have expertise. CoVenture Africa gives you the chance to turn your skills into equity — and your career into a wealth-building vehicle.

  • Choose between equity-based or milestone payment models
  • Browse vetted ventures actively seeking your skillset
  • Video profile that works for you across all opportunities
  • Be part of building — not just working for — Africa's future
  • Connect with entrepreneurs and investors who value your craft
